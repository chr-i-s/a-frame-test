Networked-Aframe AR Examples

Instructions

To view AR examples follow these steps:

  1. Grab two devices, one must have a camera
  2. Open this marker on one device
  3. Open one of the examples below on the device with a camera
  4. If other users are in the corresponding VR example, point the camera at the marker and voila
  5. If nobody is in the example, grab a third device or open a new tab and have it join the corresponding VR example
  6. Now point the camera at the marker and you should see the avatar near the marker
